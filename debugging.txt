*** Debug mobile devices with desktop ***

Android: https://developer.chrome.com/docs/devtools/remote-debugging/

1) Turn on debugging mode in your settings
2) Connect your device with USB to desktop
3) chrome://inspect/#devices
   -> copy paste above url into your desktop chrome browser
4) Open chrome browser in your mobile device
5) Then you will see the list of opened tabs of mobile chrome into your desktop chrome


iPhone: https://stackoverflow.com/questions/11262236/ios-remote-debugging

1) settings > safari > advanced
   -> turn on web inspector
2) Connect your iPhone to mac device and give permission for USB
3) Open safari browser into your desktop device and mobile device
4) In desktop desktop open the developer menu and chose the site from your iDevice (its at the top Safari Menu)
